------------------------------------

It is a requirement of The Public Sector Bodies (Websites and Mobile Applications) (No. 2) Accessibility Regulations 2018 that public sector websites and mobile applications are accessible to everyone.

What you have to do


Under the Regulations your {% include 'common/emails/templates/website-service-app-name.html' %} must meet Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG 2.2 AA standards) and we require further information.

We are asking that you provide us with the following:

[For websites]

  • Analytics on the most popular pages, forms and services
  • Examples of different templates used across the site
[For services - not top 75]
  • A short overview of the service
